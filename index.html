The Great Movies According To Bajio

My Top 8 Movies

Dirty Harry

this movie is no doubt stylish, features an eye-catching plot and has a very good
lead performance from Clint Eastwood; he really does own the role.
With that noted, I was surprised at how simply the story unfolds.

🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟

Diamonds Are Forever

DIAMONDS ARE FOREVER is a turning point in the series; the gritty,
pseudo-realism of the early films is gone in favor of slick comic book.
Bond-o-meter Rating: 81 points out of 100.

🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟

Serpico

Sweat and grit and fear pulse off it like waves; and there are dynamic
streetscape scenes. Though Pacino delivers an iconic performance.

🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟

Die Hard

A cracking Action Thriller from beginning to end, Die Hard explodes with
heart-stopping suspense! It has great cast, great plot and great explosions.

🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟

Scarface

Seriously intense and violent, but equally poetic and mesmerising.
Definately the best Al Pacino performance. Scarface has a great storyline,
brutal violence as well as having Al Pacino at one of his finest roles.

🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟

Indiana Jones And The Last Crusade

Great action and adventure. As they say third time is the charm and
they pull it out making it the second best Indiana Jones film of all time.
Plus darkness is more toned down then the last film.

🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟

Taxi Driver

Taxi Driver is so volatile it nearly explodes from the screen.
Under Martin Scorsese's brilliant direction, it works
a dramatic tension that is inescapable

🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟

Godfather

Overall, The Godfather is one of Hollywood's greatest critical and
commercial successes that gets everything right.
a gangster flick that is overflowing with life, rich with emotion and subtle acting.
